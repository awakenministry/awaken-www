Welcome to Awaken Ministry

Awaken Ministry is a monthly outreach currently hosted by various Catholic parishes in the Archdiocese of Detroit, MI. The basic format of an Awaken experience is three-fold: praise and worship, the preached Gospel, and Eucharistic Adoration. Our hope through this ministry is simply to bring people into an experiential encounter with Jesus Christ, such that they would be so touched by Godâ€™s real presence, they would be compelled to go out and share the Good News with others!
